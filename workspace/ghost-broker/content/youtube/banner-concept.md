- **Ghost icon:** Minimalist, geometric ghost shape
- **Color:** Glowing cyan/teal (#00d4ff) with subtle outer glow
- **Style:** Clean lines, tech-forward, not cute/cartoony

#### 3. Center - Text
- **Main Text:** "GHOST BROKER AI"
  - Font: Bold, modern sans-serif (Montserrat Black, Inter Bold, or similar)
  - Color: White (#ffffff)
  - Size: Dominant, readable on mobile
  
- **Tagline:** "The Invisible Hand of the Agent Economy"
  - Font: Light/Regular weight
  - Color: Light gray (#b0b8c9)
  - Size: Smaller, beneath main text

- **Options:**

## Thumbnail Style Guide (For Videos)

To maintain brand consistency across video thumbnails:

1. **Background:** Same dark navy base
2. **Text:** Bold, max 4-5 words, white or cyan
3. **Face/Subject:** If using human face, high contrast, looking at camera
4. **Brand Mark:** Small ghost icon in corner
5. **Colors:** Stick to the palette above
