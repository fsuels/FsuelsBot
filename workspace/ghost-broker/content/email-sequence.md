## A/B Testing Notes

**Subject lines to test:**
- Questions vs. statements
- Stats vs. benefits
- Curiosity gap vs. direct

**CTA variations:**
- Reply to email vs. click link
- "Free deployment" vs. "pilot project"
- Specific task examples vs. open-ended

**Tone check:**
- Maintain confident but not arrogant voice
- Keep paragraphs short (mobile-first)
- Use social proof strategically (case study, not testimonial spam)
