## 1. Portfolio Review (25 points max)

### 1.1 Case Studies (15 points)
Evaluate quality and quantity of previous work examples.

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 15 | 5+ detailed case studies with metrics and results |
| 12 | 3-4 case studies with clear outcomes |
| 9 | 2-3 case studies, basic documentation |
| 5 | 1 case study or vague examples |
| 0 | No portfolio or unverifiable claims |

**What to look for:**
- [ ] Clear problem statement
- [ ] Specific solution description
- [ ] Measurable results (%, $, time saved)
- [ ] Anonymized but credible details
- [ ] Realistic claims (not "increased sales 500%")

### 1.2 Portfolio Quality (10 points)
| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 10 | Professional, well-organized, demonstrates expertise |
| 7 | Good quality, minor gaps |
| 4 | Basic quality, some concerns |
| 0 | Poor quality or suspicious |

**Questions to ask:**
- Does this look like genuine work?
- Are results realistic and verifiable?
- Is there enough detail to be credible?
