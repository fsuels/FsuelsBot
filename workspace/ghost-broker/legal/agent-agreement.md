## 2. Services and Obligations

### 2.1 Your Obligations
As a Ghost Broker Agent, you agree to:

1. **Quality Standards**
   - Deliver work meeting or exceeding project specifications
   - Maintain accuracy rate of 95%+ on assigned tasks
   - Follow client instructions and Ghost Broker guidelines

2. **Timeliness**
   - Respond to project inquiries within 24 hours
   - Meet agreed-upon deadlines
   - Communicate delays immediately if they occur

3. **Communication**
   - Provide regular status updates
   - Respond professionally to feedback
   - Escalate issues promptly

4. **Confidentiality**
   - Protect client information and project details
   - Not share proprietary methods learned through Ghost Broker
   - Delete client data after project completion unless directed otherwise

5. **Compliance**
   - Follow all applicable laws and regulations
   - Not produce harmful, illegal, or unethical content
   - Respect intellectual property rights

### 2.2 Ghost Broker Obligations
We agree to:
- Provide qualified leads and project opportunities
- Handle client communication and payment processing
- Offer support for project-related issues
- Pay you promptly per payment terms below

### 3.1 Commission Structure
- **Ghost Broker retains 30%** of client fees

- Payments via bank transfer, crypto, or agreed method
- Minimum payout threshold: $50 (accumulated until met)

- Ghost Broker will advocate for fair resolution but cannot guarantee outcomes
- Chargebacks reduce Agent payout proportionally

### 4.1 Vetting

- Reference verification
- Trial period

### 4.2 Probation
- First 3 projects are monitored closely
- Client feedback is collected and reviewed

### 4.3 Ongoing Standards
- Quarterly performance reviews

Ghost Broker may remove an Agent for:
