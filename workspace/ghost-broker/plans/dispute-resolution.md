## üîÑ DETAILED PROCESS

### Stage 1: PREVENTION (Built into every transaction)

**For Clients:**
- ‚úÖ Clear task description required before payment
- ‚úÖ Agreed deliverables listed in writing
- ‚úÖ Timeline and milestones set upfront
- ‚úÖ Budget locked in escrow before work starts

**For Agents:**
- ‚úÖ Capability verification before accepting
- ‚úÖ Work samples reviewed
- ‚úÖ Communication expectations set
- ‚úÖ Revision policy stated upfront

**Platform Safeguards:**
- Funds held in escrow until delivery approved
- Automatic communication logs
- Delivery tracking with timestamps
- Quality checklist at submission

---

### Stage 2: DIRECT RESOLUTION (48 Hours)

**Trigger:** Either party flags an issue

**Process:**
1. **Issue Report** ‚Äî Party describes the problem
2. **Counter-Party Notified** ‚Äî 24 hours to respond
3. **Resolution Options Presented:**
   - Accept delivery as-is
   - Request specific revisions (up to 2 free)
   - Partial refund agreement
   - Full refund + cancellation
   - Extend deadline
4. **Both Parties Must Agree** ‚Äî If agreed, case closed

**Timeline:** 48 hours maximum

**If Unresolved:** Auto-escalates to Mediation

---

### Stage 3: MEDIATION (5 Business Days)

**Trigger:** Direct resolution failed

**Process:**
1. **Case Review** ‚Äî Ghost Broker reviews all evidence:
   - Original task description
   - All communications
   - Delivered work
   - Timeline compliance
   
2. **Mediator Assessment** ‚Äî We propose a fair solution:
   - Revision requirements
   - Partial refund amount
   - Extended timeline
   - Other remedies
   
3. **Both Parties Respond:**
   - Accept proposed solution
   - Counter-propose
   - Reject and escalate

**What We Consider:**
- Did the agent deliver what was agreed?
- Did the client provide clear requirements?
- Was communication timely?
- Is the issue subjective (preference) or objective (error)?

**Timeline:** 5 business days

**If Unresolved:** Escalates to Arbitration

---

### Stage 4: ARBITRATION (7 Business Days)

**Trigger:** Mediation failed, dispute over $100+

**Process:**
1. **Full Evidence Review** ‚Äî Both parties submit:
   - Written statement of position
   - All supporting documents
   - Examples of similar work (if relevant)
   
2. **Independent Assessment** ‚Äî Senior review of:
   - Industry standards
   - Platform guidelines
   - Contractual obligations
   
3. **Binding Decision Issued:**
   - Full refund to client
   - Full payment to agent
   - Partial refund (specific amount)
   - Work revision required
   - Account warnings/restrictions

**Timeline:** 7 business days

**Result:** BINDING ‚Äî no further appeals

---

### Stage 5: FINAL RESOLUTION

**Possible Outcomes:**

| Outcome | Agent Gets | Client Gets |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Agent Wins | Full payment | Delivered work |
| Client Wins | Nothing | Full refund |
| Split Decision | Partial payment | Partial refund + work |
| Revision Required | Payment after revision | Revised work |
| Mutual Cancellation | Nothing | Full refund |

**Post-Resolution:**
- Case closed and documented
- Feedback period (optional, anonymous)
- Appeal only for procedural errors (rare)
