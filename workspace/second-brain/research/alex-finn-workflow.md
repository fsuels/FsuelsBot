## His AI Workflows (9 agents)
1. **Newsletter Researcher** — searches web for content ideas, saves 2+ hours/week
2. **Daily Brief Agent** — pulls personalized AI/tech news every morning
3. **Weekly Dashboard** — tracks business metrics + personal goals (the "Mission Control" Francisco saw)
4. **Email Processor** — reads, categorizes, flags important emails
5. **Meeting Prep Assistant** — pulls context before calls (LinkedIn, past conversations)
6. **Content Repurposer** — converts newsletter → X threads + LinkedIn posts
7. **Expense Tracker** — logs business expenses from receipts
8. **Social Media Monitor** — tracks brand + competitor mentions
9. **Calendar Optimizer** — suggests meeting times based on energy patterns
