## Blog Post Template
Each post should have:
- **SEO title**: Under 60 chars, primary keyword first
- **Meta description**: Under 160 chars, keyword + CTA
- **H1**: Match SEO title
- **Word count**: 1,000-1,500 words minimum
- **Internal links**: 3-5 links to DLM product pages/collections
- **Images**: Product photos with descriptive alt text
- **CTA**: "Shop the look" buttons linking to collections
- **Schema**: FAQ schema if using Q&A format
