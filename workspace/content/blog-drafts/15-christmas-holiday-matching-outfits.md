## Christmas Matching Outfit Ideas

### Classic Red Dresses
You can't go wrong with red at Christmas. Matching red dresses — whether velvet, satin, or cotton — are timeless. Pair with black shoes and simple gold jewelry for an elegant holiday look.

### Plaid Coordinates
Buffalo plaid, tartan, or gingham in red and black is quintessential Christmas. Match in [plaid dresses or shirts](/collections/mommy-and-me-outfits) and you'll look like you stepped out of a holiday catalog.

### Green and Gold
Not everything has to be red. Rich emerald green with gold accessories is stunning and a bit unexpected. Match in green velvet and stand out from the sea of red.

### Winter White
For a modern, elegant approach, match in winter white or cream. Add metallic accessories — silver or gold — for sparkle. Just be prepared with stain remover if kids are involved.

### Ugly Christmas Sweaters
For the fun, casual holiday party: matching ugly Christmas sweaters are an absolute hit. The tackier, the better. Kids love them.

## Holiday Photo Session Tips

A Christmas photo session with matching outfits creates the ultimate holiday card. Here's how to nail it:

1. **Book your photographer in September/October** — holiday slots sell out months ahead
2. **Choose your outfits first** — then coordinate with the photo backdrop
3. **Stick to a 2-3 color palette** — red + green + white, or red + gold, or green + cream
4. **Avoid logos and busy patterns** — they distract from faces
5. **Include props** — wreaths, presents, ornaments, string lights
6. **Get multiple outfit changes** — formal, casual, and pajamas in one session
