## ğŸ† KEY INSIGHTS

### Top 5 Revenue Drivers
1. **Email flows** (15-25% of revenue runs automatically)
2. **Abandoned cart recovery** (10-15% of carts recoverable)
3. **Upsells/cross-sells** (+20-30% AOV)
4. **Reviews/social proof** (+270% conversion with reviews)
5. **Mobile optimization** (60%+ of traffic, usually 50% lower conversion)

### Quick Wins (Do This Week)
1. Set up abandoned cart email flow (1 hour, recover 10%+ of carts)
2. Add photo review incentive ($5 off = 3-5x more reviews)
3. Enable Apple Pay/Google Pay (reduce checkout friction)
4. Add "Complete the look" cross-sells (increase AOV)
5. Check mobile checkout flow (where 60% of sales should come from)

### Common Mistakes That Kill Revenue
1. No email automation (leaving 25-40% of revenue on table)
2. Ignoring mobile (where most customers are)
3. No reviews (270% conversion lift missing)
4. Shipping surprise at checkout (#1 abandonment reason)
5. Poor product photos (customers can't buy what they can't see)
