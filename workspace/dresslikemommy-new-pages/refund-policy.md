# Refund Policy — Dress Like Mommy (DRAFT)

## Returns & Exchanges

We want you and your family to love your matching outfits! If something isn't right, we're here to help.

### Return Window

You have **30 days** from the date of delivery to request a return or exchange.

### Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, items must be:
- Unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
- In their original packaging with all tags attached
- Free of stains, pet hair, perfume, or signs of wear

### Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:
- Swimwear and intimates (for hygiene reasons)
- Items marked as "Final Sale"
- Gift cards
- Items returned after 30 days

### How to Start a Return

1. **Email us** at info@dresslikemommy.com with your order number and reason for return
2. Our team will review your request and respond within 1 business day
3. We'll provide you with return instructions and a return shipping address
4. Ship the item(s) back to us in their original packaging

### Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return:
- You'll receive an email confirming we received your item
- If approved, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within **5-10 business days**
- Depending on your bank or credit card company, it may take an additional 2-5 business days to appear on your statement

### Exchanges

Want a different size or color? Email us at info@dresslikemommy.com with your order number and we'll help you get the right item.

### Damaged or Defective Items

Received a damaged or defective item? Contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos of the issue. We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund — no need to return the damaged item.

### Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within 10 business days of our confirmation email:
1. Check your bank account or credit card statement again
2. Contact your credit card company — processing times vary
3. Contact your bank — there may be additional processing time
4. If you've done all this, email us at info@dresslikemommy.com and we'll investigate

### Shipping Costs

- Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the item arrived damaged or defective
- Original shipping costs are non-refundable
