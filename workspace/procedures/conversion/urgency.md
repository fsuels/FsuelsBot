## Common Mistakes

### âŒ Avoid These

1. **Fake scarcity** - Illegal and trust-destroying
2. **Perpetual sales** - "Sale always ending today"
3. **Aggressive popups** - Multiple urgency popups annoy
4. **Timer resets** - Obvious deception
5. **Unrealistic claims** - "Only 1 left!" on every product
6. **Constant urgency** - If everything is urgent, nothing is
7. **No follow-through** - Sale extends indefinitely
8. **Mobile neglect** - Urgency elements must work on mobile
9. **Wrong audience** - Luxury buyers may find urgency cheap
10. **Ignoring feedback** - If customers complain, listen

   - Based on their actual shipping location
   - Requires accurate time zone detection

## Legal Reminder

### FTC Guidelines (US)
- Discounts must be from genuine regular prices
- Scarcity claims must be true
- Time-limited offers must actually end
- "Previously sold at" prices must be accurate

### Best Practice
- Document your promotions and inventory
- Be able to prove any claim made
- When in doubt, err on side of transparency
- Consult legal counsel for major campaigns
