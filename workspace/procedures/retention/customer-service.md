## Escalation Protocol

**Level 1: Standard CS**
- Size questions, tracking, simple returns

**Level 2: Supervisor**
- Angry customers
- Refund disputes
- Social media complaints

**Level 3: Owner/Manager**
- Legal threats
- PR issues
- Large order problems
