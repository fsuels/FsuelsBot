# Reviews Collection Procedure

## Revenue Impact
- Products with reviews convert **270% higher** than those without
- Photo reviews drive **3-5x higher conversion**
- Each review = social proof that sells for you 24/7

## Verification Gate
- [ ] Review app installed (Okendo, Loox, Judge.me)
- [ ] Post-purchase email flow active
- [ ] Review display on product pages
- [ ] Photo review incentive set

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### 1. Request Reviews (Automated)

### 2. Incentivize Photo Reviews

| Action | Reward |
|--------|--------|
| Text review | Thank you email |
| Photo review | $5 discount code |
| Video review | $10 discount code |

Photo reviews are worth the discount - they convert browsers into buyers.

### 3. Display Reviews for Maximum Impact

**On Product Pages**
- Star rating in title area (above fold)
- Total review count visible
- Photo reviews in image gallery
- Full reviews section below
- Filter by rating, size, photos

**Across Site**
- Homepage featured reviews
- Collection pages show ratings
- Cart page reassurance

### 4. Respond to ALL Reviews

**Positive Reviews**
```
Thank you so much, [Name]! üíï We love seeing you and your mini matching! 
Tag us @dresslikemommy in your photos - we'd love to feature you!
```

**Negative Reviews**
```
We're so sorry to hear this, [Name]. That's not the experience we want for you.
Please email us at support@... and we'll make it right immediately.
```

Responding shows you care AND gives SEO-boosting fresh content.
