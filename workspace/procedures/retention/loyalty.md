## Program Structures

### Option 1: Points-Based System

**How It Works**
```
Earn: 1 point per $1 spent
Redeem: 100 points = $5 off

Bonus points:
- First purchase: 50 bonus points
- Birthday: 100 points
- Review with photo: 50 points
- Referral: 200 points
```

**Pros**: Simple, familiar, flexible
**Cons**: Can feel transactional, points devalue over time

### Option 2: Tiered VIP Program

**Tiers**
| Tier | Requirement | Benefits |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Member** | Sign up | 5% back in points, birthday reward |
| **Silver** | 2+ orders or $150+ | 10% back, early access |
| **Gold** | 4+ orders or $300+ | 15% back, free shipping, exclusive |
| **Platinum** | 8+ orders or $500+ | 20% back, VIP support, surprises |

**Pros**: Creates aspiration, tiered engagement
**Cons**: More complex, higher tier customers expect more

### Option 3: Paid Membership

**Structure**
```
Join for $29/year and get:
- 15% off everything, always
- Free shipping on all orders
- Early access to sales (48h)
- Birthday gift
- Exclusive member-only products
```

**Pros**: Immediate revenue, high commitment
**Cons**: Barrier to entry, requires strong value prop

### Recommendation for Mommy-and-Me
**Tiered VIP + Points Hybrid**
- Points for earning/redeeming
- Tiers for status and escalating benefits
- Matches the aspirational, family-focused brand

## Implementation Steps

### Phase 1: Setup (Week 1-2)

1. **Choose Loyalty App**
   - Smile.io ($49-199/mo)
   - LoyaltyLion ($159-699/mo)
   - Yotpo Loyalty (Enterprise)
   - Stamped.io ($59-199/mo)

2. **Configure Program**
   - Set point earning rules
   - Define redemption options
   - Design tier structure
   - Set up email notifications

3. **Design Assets**
   - Program explainer page
   - Tier badges/icons
   - Email templates
   - Social graphics

## Common Mistakes

### ‚ùå Avoid These

1. **Too complicated** - Keep it simple to understand
2. **Hard to earn** - Make first reward achievable fast
3. **Worthless rewards** - Rewards must feel valuable
4. **No communication** - Remind people of their points
5. **Points expire too fast** - 12+ month expiration
6. **Hidden program** - Promote it everywhere
7. **No tier benefits** - Higher tiers need real perks
8. **Ignoring data** - Track and optimize
9. **Set and forget** - Programs need ongoing attention
10. **Copying competitors** - Design for YOUR customers

## Expert Tips

### üéØ Advanced Strategies

1. **Surprise and Delight**
   - Random "just because" rewards
   - Handwritten notes for VIPs
   - Unexpected tier upgrades

2. **Gamification**
   - Progress bars to next tier
   - "You're 50 points from $10 off!"
   - Streak bonuses for consecutive months

3. **Experiential Rewards**
   - Early access to launches
   - Exclusive products
   - "Design the next collection" contest

4. **Social Proof in Program**
   - "Join 10,000+ Mama Members"
   - Show tier badges on reviews
   - Community features

5. **Birthday Program**
   - Capture birthday at signup
   - Send reward 7 days before
   - Make it feel special, not generic

6. **Referral Integration**
   - Give points for referrals
   - Give referred friend points too
   - Double sided = higher participation
