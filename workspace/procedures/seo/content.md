# Content SEO Procedure

> **Verification Gate:** Before proceeding, state: "I have read the content SEO procedure. Content strategy focuses on: informational keywords, building authority, and supporting product pages with internal links."

## Purpose
Create blog content that attracts organic traffic, builds authority, and supports product sales.

## Prerequisites
- Keyword research completed (informational keywords identified)
- Shopify blog set up
- Understanding of target audience

## Tools/Resources
- Shopify Admin > Online Store > Blog posts
- Keyword map (informational keywords)
- Competitor blogs for topic ideas

---

## Step-by-Step Procedure

### Part 1: Content Strategy

#### Step 1: Identify Content Topics
Content should serve these purposes:
- [ ] **Informational:** Answer questions your customers ask
- [ ] **Inspirational:** Outfit ideas, photo tips, lifestyle content
- [ ] **Seasonal:** Holiday outfit guides, seasonal trends
- [ ] **Educational:** Style guides, sizing help, care tips

#### Step 2: Map Topics to Keywords
Target informational keywords (from keyword research):
- [ ] "mommy and me photo outfit ideas"
- [ ] "what to wear for mother daughter pictures"
- [ ] "how to style matching family outfits"
- [ ] "best mommy and me outfits for [occasion]"

#### Step 3: Create Content Calendar
Plan content around:
- [ ] **Seasonal moments:** Valentine's (late January), Easter (March), Summer (May), Back to School (July), Halloween (September), Christmas (November)
- [ ] **Evergreen content:** Style guides, sizing guides, care tips
- [ ] **Frequency:** Minimum 2-4 posts per month

---

### Part 2: Writing SEO-Optimized Blog Posts

#### Step 4: Structure Your Post
**Standard blog post structure:**

```markdown
# [Keyword-Rich Title - Under 60 chars]

[Opening paragraph - include primary keyword in first 100 words]

## [H2 Subheading with Secondary Keyword]

[Content section - 150-300 words]

## [H2 Subheading]

[Content section with bullet points or numbered lists]

### [H3 Subheading for sub-topics]

[More detailed content]

## Conclusion

[Summary with CTA and link to products]
```

#### Step 5: Write the Post
For each blog post:
- [ ] **Title:** Include primary keyword, under 60 characters
  - Good: "10 Adorable Valentine's Day Mommy and Me Outfit Ideas"
  - Bad: "Outfit Ideas"
- [ ] **Opening:** Hook reader + include keyword naturally
- [ ] **Body:** Use H2/H3 subheadings with keywords, bullet lists, short paragraphs
- [ ] **Length:** Minimum 800 words (1500+ for pillar content)
- [ ] **Images:** Include 3-5 images with alt text
- [ ] **Internal links:** Link to 2-3 relevant products/collections
- [ ] **CTA:** Clear call-to-action with link to shop

#### Step 6: Optimize Meta Tags
- [ ] Go to Blog posts > [Post] > Edit website SEO
- [ ] **Title tag:** `[Post Title] | Dress Like Mommy Blog`
- [ ] **Meta description:** Summarize value, include keyword, add CTA (150-160 chars)
- [ ] **URL handle:** `valentines-mommy-me-outfit-ideas` (clean, keyword-rich)

#### Step 7: Add Internal Links
Within each post:
- [ ] Link to 2-3 relevant products
- [ ] Link to relevant collection pages
- [ ] Link to related blog posts
- [ ] Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")

### Part 3: Content Types for E-commerce

#### Outfit Idea Posts
**Template:**
```
Title: [Number] [Occasion] Mommy and Me Outfit Ideas

Intro: Brief intro about the occasion + why matching matters

[Outfit 1: Description + Photo + Link to Product]
[Outfit 2: Description + Photo + Link to Product]
...

Tips: Styling tips, accessorizing, photo tips

CTA: Shop our [occasion] collection
```

#### Buying Guides
**Template:**
```
Title: How to Choose the Perfect [Product Type] for Mommy and Me

Intro: Why this product matters + what to look for

What to Consider:
- Fabric and comfort
- Sizing across ages
- Occasion appropriateness
- Budget

Top Picks: [Links to products]

CTA: Browse our [collection]
```

#### Seasonal Content
**Template:**
```
Title: [Season/Holiday] Mommy and Me Outfit Guide [Year]

Intro: What's trending this season

Trend 1: [Description + product links]
Trend 2: [Description + product links]
Trend 3: [Description + product links]

CTA: Shop [seasonal collection]
```

## Quality Criteria
✅ Post is 800+ words (1500+ for pillar content)  
✅ Primary keyword in title, URL, first paragraph  
✅ H2/H3 structure with keyword variations  
✅ 3-5 images with alt text  
✅ 2-3 internal links to products/collections  
✅ Meta title under 60 chars, description 150-160 chars  
✅ Clear CTA with link to shop  

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
❌ Thin content (under 300 words)  
❌ No internal links to products  
❌ Missing images or images without alt text  
❌ Duplicate content (copying from other sites)  
❌ Keyword stuffing (unnatural repetition)  
❌ No clear CTA or next step  
❌ Ignoring meta tags (using defaults)  

---

## Content Publishing Workflow

1. **Research:** Identify keyword + topic (30 min)
2. **Outline:** Create structure with H2/H3 (15 min)
3. **Write:** Draft content (1-2 hours)
4. **Optimize:** Add images, internal links, meta tags (30 min)
5. **Review:** Proofread, check links work (15 min)
6. **Publish:** Set live or schedule
7. **Promote:** Share on social media

## Shopify Blog Path
- Online Store > Blog posts > Add blog post
- Rich text editor for content
- Featured image on right side
