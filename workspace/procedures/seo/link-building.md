# Link Building Procedure

> **Verification Gate:** Before proceeding, state: "I have read the link building procedure. Quality over quantity: one link from a relevant parenting site is worth more than 100 low-quality directory links."

## Purpose
Build backlinks from relevant, authoritative sites to improve domain authority and rankings.

## Prerequisites
- Quality content on site (linkable assets)
- Social media presence
- Understanding of ethical link building (no buying links, no spam)

## Tools/Resources
- **Free backlink checkers:** Ahrefs free backlink checker, Ubersuggest
- **Outreach:** Gmail, social media DMs
- **Competitor analysis:** See where competitors get links

---

## Step-by-Step Procedure

### Part 1: Understand Your Link Profile

#### Step 1: Check Current Backlinks
- [ ] Go to ahrefs.com/backlink-checker (free version)
- [ ] Enter your domain: dresslikemommy.com
- [ ] Note: Total backlinks, referring domains, domain rating
- [ ] Identify existing good links (relevant sites, real sites)

#### Step 2: Analyze Competitor Links
- [ ] Check 3-5 competitor domains
- [ ] Note where they get links from
- [ ] Identify opportunities (sites that link to them but not you)

---

### Part 2: Link Building Strategies for E-commerce

#### Strategy 1: Product Reviews & Gifting

**Step 3: Identify Mommy Bloggers**
- [ ] Search: "mommy blogger reviews" + "mom blog fashion"
- [ ] Look for bloggers who review products
- [ ] Check their domain authority and audience fit
- [ ] Make list of 20-30 potential bloggers

**Step 4: Outreach for Reviews**
- [ ] Send personalized email or DM
- [ ] Offer free product in exchange for honest review
- [ ] Don't require they link (but most will)
- [ ] Follow up after 2 weeks if no response

**Template:**
```
Subject: Mommy and Me Sets for [Blog Name]?

Hi [Name],

I love your content on [specific post they wrote]. Your style is exactly the kind of mom we love at Dress Like Mommy.

I'd love to send you and your mini a matching set from our [collection]. No strings attached - just hoping you'll love it enough to share with your followers!

Would you be interested? Happy to send a few options to choose from.

[Your name]
Dress Like Mommy
```

#### Strategy 2: Pinterest & Visual Content

**Step 5: Create Shareable Content**
- [ ] Create beautiful product flat-lays
- [ ] Create outfit idea graphics
- [ ] Create seasonal mood boards
- [ ] Add to Pinterest with link back to site

**Step 6: Enable Rich Pins**
- [ ] Set up Rich Pins for products
- [ ] Products show price, availability on Pinterest
- [ ] Links drive traffic back to site

#### Strategy 3: Parent Community Engagement

**Step 7: Participate in Mom Communities**
- [ ] Join Facebook groups for moms
- [ ] Join parenting forums
- [ ] Be helpful, not promotional
- [ ] Add value first, link occasionally when relevant

**Step 8: Answer Questions**
- [ ] Find questions about mommy-and-me outfits on:
  - Quora
  - Reddit (r/Mommit, r/parenting)
  - Facebook groups
- [ ] Give genuinely helpful answers
- [ ] Mention your brand naturally when relevant

#### Strategy 4: Content-Based Links

**Step 9: Create Linkable Assets**
Create content others want to link to:
- [ ] "Ultimate Guide to Mommy and Me Outfits" (comprehensive resource)
- [ ] "Mother Daughter Activities Guide" (broader appeal)
- [ ] Infographics (outfit styling tips, size charts)
- [ ] Free printables (photo checklists, packing lists)

**Step 10: Guest Posting**
- [ ] Identify parenting/lifestyle blogs that accept guest posts
- [ ] Pitch relevant topic ideas
- [ ] Write quality content with natural link back
- [ ] Don't over-promote in the post

**Guest Post Pitch Template:**
```
Subject: Guest Post Idea for [Blog Name]

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your name] from Dress Like Mommy. I've been following [blog] for a while and love your content on [topic].

I'd love to contribute a guest post on [topic idea]. I was thinking something like:

"[Proposed Title]" - covering [brief outline of what you'd write]

This would be original content written just for your readers. Let me know if you're interested!

Best,
[Name]
```

#### Strategy 5: Local & Niche Opportunities

**Step 11: Local Business Links**
- [ ] Join local Chamber of Commerce (often has directory)
- [ ] Partner with local photographers (mommy-and-me shoots)
- [ ] Connect with local mom groups

**Step 12: Niche Directories**
Submit to relevant directories (only quality ones):
- [ ] Mommy blogger networks
- [ ] Small business directories
- [ ] Shopify store directories
- [ ] Industry-specific lists

### Part 3: Link Building Don'ts (Critical)

#### What NOT to Do
- ❌ Buy links (Google penalizes this)
- ❌ Link exchange schemes ("I'll link to you if you link to me")
- ❌ Spam blog comments with links
- ❌ Submit to hundreds of low-quality directories
- ❌ Use automated link building tools
- ❌ Get links from irrelevant sites (casino sites, pharma, etc.)

## Quality Criteria
✅ Links from relevant sites (parenting, fashion, lifestyle)  
✅ Links from real sites with real traffic  
✅ Diverse link profile (not all from one source)  
✅ Natural anchor text variation  
✅ No paid links or link schemes  
✅ Tracking outreach and results  

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
❌ Focusing on quantity over quality  
❌ Buying links or using link farms  
❌ Getting links from irrelevant sites  
❌ Over-optimized anchor text ("click here for best mommy and me outfits")  
❌ Spamming forums and comments  
❌ Expecting instant results (link building takes months)  

---

## Realistic Expectations

- **Timeline:** 3-6 months to see ranking improvements from links
- **Effort:** 2-4 hours per week on outreach and relationship building
- **Results:** Focus on 5-10 quality links per month, not 100 low-quality ones
- **Measurement:** Track referring domains monthly in Search Console

- [ ] Follow up on previous outreach

Week 3:
- [ ] Engage in 3-5 community discussions
- [ ] Pitch 2 guest posts

Week 4:
- [ ] Review backlink profile
- [ ] Disavow any spammy links found
