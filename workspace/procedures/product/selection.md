# Product Selection Procedure

## Revenue Impact
Right products = sales. Wrong products = dead inventory.
This is the most important decision you make.

---

## Selection Criteria

### Must-Have Attributes

| Criteria | Requirement | Why |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| Visual appeal | Photographs beautifully | Photos sell online |
| Margin | 50%+ after all costs | Profitability |
| Demand signal | Proven sellers exist | Market validation |
| Size range | Mom + Child sizes | Our niche |
| Quality | Customer won't return | Low return rate |
| Shipping | Reasonable weight/size | Cost control |

### Scoring System (1-5 each)

| Factor | Weight | Score |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Market demand | 25% | /5 |
| Profit margin | 25% | /5 |
| Photo potential | 20% | /5 |
| Quality signals | 15% | /5 |
| Uniqueness | 15% | /5 |

**Minimum score to add: 3.5/5**

## Finding Products

### Step 1: Trend Research

**Where to look**:
- Pinterest Trends (what's being saved)
- TikTok "mommy and me" hashtag
- Amazon bestsellers (matching family)
- Competitor new arrivals
- Seasonal planning (2-3 months ahead)

**Signals of demand**:
- Multiple sellers offering similar
- High engagement on social posts
- Seasonal relevance coming
- Solves clear need (photos, holidays)

### Step 2: Sourcing (See vendor-vetting.md)

1. Search 1688.com for product
2. Evaluate 3-5 vendors
3. Select best quality/price/reviews
4. Import via BuckyDrop

### Step 3: Validation

**Before adding to store**:
- [ ] Can we hit 50% margin?
- [ ] Photos look good?
- [ ] Size range complete?
- [ ] Vendor reliable (see reviews)?
- [ ] Not already saturated in our store?

### Step 4: Test Launch

1. List 1-3 variations
2. Run small ad test ($20-50)
3. Measure CTR and conversion
4. Scale winners, cut losers

## Product Mix

### Category Balance

| Category | Target % | Why |
|----------|----------|-----|
| Dresses | 30% | High AOV, hero products |
| Casual (tees, sweats) | 30% | Everyday sellers |
| Seasonal | 20% | Time-sensitive demand |
| Accessories | 15% | Upsells, AOV boost |
| Special occasion | 5% | High margin, lower volume |

### Seasonal Calendar

| Month | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| Jan-Feb | Valentine's, Spring transition |
| Mar-Apr | Easter, Spring, Mother's Day |
| May-Jun | Summer, vacation wear |
| Jul-Aug | Back to school, Fall preview |
| Sep-Oct | Fall, Halloween |
| Nov-Dec | Holiday, Christmas pajamas |

**Plan 8-12 weeks ahead for seasonal drops.**

## Killing Products

Remove products that:
- No sales in 60 days
- High return rate (>20%)
- Consistent quality complaints
- Low margin after discounts
- Supplier issues

**How to kill**:
1. Stop ads
2. Discount to clear (30-50% off)
3. Remove when sold through
4. Learn why it failed
