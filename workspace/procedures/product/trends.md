# Trend Spotting Procedure

## Revenue Impact
Be early on trends = premium pricing, less competition.
Be late = race to bottom on price.

---

## Where to Spot Trends

### Primary Sources (Check Weekly)

| Source | What to Look For |
|--------|------------------|

| TikTok | Viral matching outfits, sounds |

## Trend Types

### Macro Trends (12-24 months)
- Color of the year (Pantone)
- Silhouette shifts
- Sustainability focus
- Material trends

**Action**: Inform overall direction

### Micro Trends (3-6 months)
- Specific patterns (hearts, florals)
- Holiday themes
- Viral styles
- Celebrity influence

**Action**: Quick product adds, seasonal

### Fads (1-4 weeks)
- TikTok viral moments
- Meme-driven
- Very short window

**Action**: Usually skip - too fast for our model

---

## Trend Validation

Before investing in a trend:

1. **Search volume check** (Google Trends)
   - Is it actually rising?
   - How long has it been trending?

2. **Competition check**
   - Are big players doing it?
   - Is there room for us?

3. **Timing check**
   - Can we source in time?
   - How long will trend last?

4. **Fit check**
   - Does it match our brand?
   - Will our customer want it?

## Seasonal Planning Calendar

### Q1 (Jan-Mar)
- **January**: Valentine's products live, Spring sourcing
- **February**: Valentine's peak, Easter sourcing
- **March**: Easter products live, Mother's Day sourcing

### Q2 (Apr-Jun)
- **April**: Mother's Day products live, Summer sourcing
- **May**: Mother's Day peak, 4th of July sourcing
- **June**: Summer live, Back to school sourcing

### Q3 (Jul-Sep)
- **July**: Summer peak, Fall sourcing
- **August**: Back to school, Halloween sourcing
- **September**: Fall live, Holiday sourcing

### Q4 (Oct-Dec)
- **October**: Halloween peak, Holiday products live
- **November**: Holiday push begins
- **December**: Holiday peak, Valentine's sourcing (loop)

**Rule**: Source 8-12 weeks before you need to sell.
