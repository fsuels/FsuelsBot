## Influencer Strategy Overview

### Why Influencers for Mommy-and-Me
- **Trust**: Moms trust other moms
- **Visual Proof**: Matching outfits need to be SEEN
- **Authenticity**: Real families > studio shots
- **UGC Factory**: Get content AND promotion

### Influencer Tiers

| Tier | Followers | Cost | Best For |
|------|-----------|------|----------|
| Nano | 1K-10K | Free product | Authentic UGC, volume |
| Micro | 10K-50K | $50-$300 | Best ROI, engaged audiences |
| Mid | 50K-250K | $300-$2,000 | Reach + credibility |
| Macro | 250K-1M | $2,000-$10,000 | Brand awareness |
| Mega | 1M+ | $10,000+ | Rarely worth it for small brands |

### Where to Focus
**80% budget on Nano + Micro influencers**
- Higher engagement rates (3-10% vs <2% for big influencers)
- More authentic content
- Lower risk per partnership
- Can run many simultaneously

## Step-by-Step Process

### Phase 1: Finding Influencers

1. **Hashtag Mining**
   - Search: #mommyandme, #twinningwithmommy, #motherdaughter
   - Look at who's posting, not who's tagged
   - Note engagement rates, not just followers

2. **Competitor Analysis**
   - Who's posting about similar brands?
   - Check competitor tagged photos
   - Look at who they follow

3. **Platform-Specific Search**
   
   **Instagram**
   - Use native search with location filters
   - Check Reels creators in your niche
   - Look at who your ideal customers follow
   
   **TikTok**
   - Search trending hashtags
   - Find creators with viral mommy content
   - Check "For You" page patterns
   
   **Pinterest**
   - Look at top pinners in your categories
   - Check Idea Pin creators
   - Review who's sharing product content

4. **Tools for Discovery**
   - **Modash** ($99/mo) - Database search
   - **Heepsy** ($49/mo) - Influencer discovery
   - **Grin** ($Enterprise) - Full platform
   - **Manual search** - Free, time-intensive

### Phase 4: Partnership Setup

1. **Define Deliverables**
   - Number of posts (1-3)
   - Format (Reel, Stories, static post, TikTok)
   - Timeline (when to post)
   - Caption requirements (tags, hashtags, disclosure)
   - Usage rights (can you use their content?)

2. **Compensation Options**
   
   | Type | Best For | Pros | Cons |
   |------|----------|------|------|
   | Gifted | Nano/Micro | Low cost | No guarantee |
   | Flat Fee | All sizes | Predictable | Upfront cost |
   | Affiliate | Engaged audiences | Performance-based | Lower effort from influencer |
   | Hybrid | Micro/Mid | Balance | Complexity |

3. **Contract Must-Haves**
   - Deliverables and deadlines
   - FTC disclosure requirements (#ad, #sponsored)
   - Usage rights (where you can repost)
   - Exclusivity period (if any)
   - Content approval process
   - Payment terms
   - Cancellation terms

4. **Discount Code Setup**
   - Unique code per influencer (INFLUENCERNAME15)
   - Track code usage in Shopify
   - Typical: 15-20% off for their audience
   - Commission: 10-20% of code sales (if affiliate)

### Phase 5: Execution

1. **Product Selection**
   - Let influencer choose (invested in content)
   - OR curate based on their aesthetic
   - Include size options for perfect fit
   - Add handwritten note

2. **Shipping & Tracking**
   - Send with tracking
   - Confirm receipt
   - Set deadline for content (2-4 weeks after receipt)

3. **Content Review**
   - Request draft/preview before posting (if in contract)
   - Check for brand alignment
   - Verify FTC disclosure present
   - Provide feedback kindly

4. **Post-Posting**
   - Engage immediately (like, comment, share)
   - Share to your Stories
   - Save content for repurposing
   - Track performance

### Phase 6: Measurement & Optimization

**Track Per Influencer**
- Discount code redemptions
- Revenue generated
- UTM link clicks
- Follower gain during campaign
- Content engagement
- Quality of UGC received

**Calculate ROI**
```
Influencer ROI = (Revenue from code + Value of UGC) / Total Cost

Example:
Revenue: $500 from 10 orders using their code
UGC Value: $200 (content you'd pay for otherwise)
Total Cost: $150 (product + shipping)
ROI = ($500 + $200) / $150 = 4.7x
```

## Expert Tips ($10M Store Tactics)

### ðŸŽ¯ Advanced Strategies

1. **Ambassador Program > One-off Partnerships**
   - Long-term relationships = better content
   - Build a "mom squad" of 10-20 ambassadors
   - Monthly requirements, ongoing perks
   - Authentic advocacy over time

2. **Content Mining**
   - Every influencer partnership = content factory
   - Use their photos in ads (with permission)
   - Use in emails, website, social
   - UGC often outperforms branded content

3. **Affiliate + Flat Fee Hybrid**
   - Pay $100 flat + 15% commission
   - Aligned incentives = better promotion
   - They're invested in sales, not just posting

4. **Seeding at Scale**
   - Send product to 50+ nano influencers/month
   - No formal ask, just "we love your style"
   - Many will post organically
   - Build goodwill and awareness

5. **Event/Campaign Moments**
   - Coordinate multiple influencers for launch
   - Same day = amplification effect
   - Creates trending moment

6. **The "Surprise and Delight" Box**
   - Over-deliver on packaging
   - Include handwritten note
   - Add unexpected bonus item
   - Creates better unboxing content

7. **Re-engage Past Partners**
   - Check in every 2-3 months
   - Send new products
   - Offer exclusive first access
   - 10x easier than finding new influencers

8. **Track Beyond Last-Click**
   - Influencers drive awareness, not just direct sales
   - Look at branded search lift
   - Track social follower growth
   - Measure UGC quality and quantity
