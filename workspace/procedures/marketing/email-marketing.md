## Step-by-Step Process

### List Building (Ongoing)

1. **Pop-up Optimization**
   - Trigger: 5-10 seconds OR 50% scroll OR exit intent
   - Offer: 10-15% first purchase OR free shipping
   - Design: Match brand, mobile-friendly
   - A/B test: Headline, offer amount, design

2. **Collection Points**
   - Homepage pop-up (main driver)
   - Footer signup form
   - Checkout checkbox
   - SMS + Email combo capture
   - Post-purchase: "Get early access"
   - Quiz: "Find your matching style"

3. **Lead Magnets That Work**
   - "10 Ways to Style Mommy-and-Me Outfits" guide
   - "Size Guide for Perfect Matching" PDF
   - "Exclusive VIP Sale Access" promise
   - "Birthday Club" signup

### Campaign Calendar

**Weekly Cadence (minimum)**
| Day | Email Type | Goal |
|-----|-----------|------|
| Tuesday | Value/Content | Open rate |
| Thursday | Promotional | Sales |
| Saturday | Flash/Weekend | Urgency |

**Monthly Themes**
- Week 1: New arrivals focus
- Week 2: Best sellers + social proof
- Week 3: Educational content
- Week 4: End-of-month promo

### Campaign Creation Process

1. **Planning (Monday)**
   - Review this week's promotions
   - Check inventory (don't promote low stock unless intentional)
   - Write subject lines (3 variations)
   - Outline email structure

2. **Creation (Tuesday AM)**
   - Design in Canva or use ESP templates
   - Write copy: Hook â†’ Story â†’ Offer â†’ CTA
   - Add product blocks (4-8 products)
   - Set preview text (50-100 chars)

3. **QA Checklist (Before Send)**
   - [ ] Links all work
   - [ ] Images load on mobile
   - [ ] Subject line <50 characters
   - [ ] Preview text compelling
   - [ ] CTA button prominent
   - [ ] Unsubscribe link present
   - [ ] Send test to yourself

4. **Send Optimization**
   - Best times: 10 AM, 2 PM, 8 PM (test for your list)
   - Best days: Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday
   - A/B test send times monthly

5. **Post-Send Analysis (24 hours after)**
   - Open rate: Target >25%
   - Click rate: Target >3%
   - Revenue: Track per email
   - Unsubscribes: Flag if >0.5%

## Essential Email Flows (Automation)

### 1. Welcome Series (CRITICAL)
**Goal: Convert new subscriber â†’ first customer**

**Goal: Recover 10-15% of abandoned carts**

| Email | Timing | Content | Offer |
|-------|--------|---------|-------|
| 1 | 1 hour | "Forgot something?" + cart contents | None |
| 2 | 24 hours | Social proof + answer objections | None |
| 3 | 48 hours | Discount code (5-10%) | Yes |
| 4 | 72 hours | Last chance + scarcity | Yes |

### 3. Browse Abandonment
**Goal: Re-engage window shoppers**

| Email | Timing | Content |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 1 | 2 hours | "Still thinking about this?" + viewed product |
| 2 | 24 hours | Similar products + best sellers |

### 4. Post-Purchase (RETENTION GOLD)
**Goal: Turn one-time buyers into repeat customers**

| Email | Timing | Content |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Immediate | Order confirmation + cross-sells |
| 2 | Day 3 | Shipping notification + "what to expect" |
| 3 | Day 7 | How-to style your new matching outfits |
| 4 | Day 14 | Review request + photo submission incentive |
| 5 | Day 30 | Loyalty program invite + next purchase offer |
| 6 | Day 45 | Complementary products + "Complete the look" |

### 5. Win-Back Flow
**Goal: Re-engage lapsed customers**

| Email | Timing | Content | Offer |
|-------|--------|---------|-------|
| 1 | 60 days | "We miss you" + new arrivals | None |
| 2 | 75 days | Best sellers + what's changed | 10% off |

### 6. VIP/Loyalty Flows
- Early access to sales (24-48 hours before public)
- Birthday emails (discount + free shipping)
- Anniversary emails (first purchase date)
- Milestone emails (3rd, 5th, 10th order)

## Segmentation Strategy

### Must-Have Segments

| Segment | Definition | Use |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| Engaged | Opened/clicked in 30 days | Send everything |
| Semi-engaged | Opened in 60 days, not 30 | Re-engagement focus |
| At-risk | No opens in 60-90 days | Win-back flows |
| VIP | 3+ orders OR $500+ spent | Early access, exclusives |
| First-timers | 1 order only | Second purchase push |
| High-AOV | Orders $100+ | Premium product focus |
| Sale shoppers | Only buys on discount | Promo-only sends |
| Full-price | Buys without discount | Avoid discounting |

### Advanced Segments
- By product category purchased
- By size purchased (predict next needs)
- By season purchased (retarget annually)
- By source (social, organic, ads)

## Common Mistakes

### âŒ Avoid These
1. **Buying email lists** - Destroys deliverability, illegal (GDPR/CAN-SPAM)
2. **Sending to everyone** - Kills open rates, triggers spam filters
3. **No segmentation** - One-size emails don't convert
4. **Ignoring mobile** - 60%+ opens are mobile
5. **Walls of text** - Emails should be scannable
6. **Weak subject lines** - 80% of success is getting opened
7. **No automation** - Flows should run while you sleep
8. **Over-discounting** - Trains customers to wait for sales
9. **Not cleaning list** - Remove non-openers every 90 days
10. **Single CTA** - Have one clear action per email

## Expert Tips ($10M Store Tactics)

### ðŸŽ¯ Advanced Strategies

1. **The 80/20 Subject Line Rule**
   - 80% of opens are decided by subject line
   - Test: Question vs Statement, Emoji vs No emoji, Length
   - Keep swipe file of winners

2. **Plain Text Emails Work**
   - For win-back and personal touches
   - Higher deliverability
   - Feels like real email from friend

3. **Preview Text is Second Subject Line**
   - Don't let it default to "View in browser"
   - Use it to complete the subject line hook

4. **Sunset Non-Engaged Subscribers**
   - After 120 days no opens â†’ send final win-back
   - Then remove from list
   - Quality > Quantity for deliverability

5. **The "Founder Email" Hack**
   - Plain text emails "from the founder"
   - Personal story + offer
   - 2-3x higher reply rates

6. **Resend to Non-Openers**
   - 48-72 hours later with new subject line
   - Easy 10-20% more opens

7. **Zero-Party Data Collection**
   - Ask preferences in flows
   - "Are you shopping for mom or child sizes?"
   - "What's your style: casual or dressy?"
   - Use for hyper-personalization

8. **Post-Purchase Upsell Timing**
   - Best time to sell: right after purchase
   - Add order bump in confirmation email
   - "Add X to your order before it ships"

## Emergency Playbook

### If deliverability drops:
1. Stop all campaigns immediately
2. Check spam score with mail-tester.com
3. Clean list aggressively (60-day non-openers)
4. Send only to most engaged for 2 weeks
5. Review authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)

### If open rates crater:
1. Check if landed in Promotions/Spam
2. Try plain text email
3. Test radically different subject lines
4. Send at different time
5. Segment to engaged only, rebuild trust
