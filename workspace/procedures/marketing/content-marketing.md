## Step-by-Step Process

### Blog Post Creation

**Step 1: Keyword Research**
1. Use keyword tools (Ubersuggest, Ahrefs, SEMrush)
2. Target keywords with 100-10K monthly searches
3. Check competition (can you rank?)
4. Map intent: informational vs commercial

**Step 2: Outline**
```
- H1: Primary keyword in title
- Intro: Hook + preview (100-150 words)
- H2: Main section 1 (with secondary keyword)
  - H3: Subsection
  - H3: Subsection
- H2: Main section 2
- H2: Main section 3
- H2: FAQ section (featured snippet opportunity)
- Conclusion: Summary + CTA
```

**Step 3: Write**
- Target 1,500-2,500 words for SEO posts
- Use conversational tone
- Include internal links (3-5 to products/other posts)
- Add external links (1-2 to authoritative sources)
- Include product recommendations naturally

**Step 4: Optimize**
- Meta title: Primary keyword, <60 characters
- Meta description: Compelling, keyword, <160 characters
- URL: Short, keyword-focused
- Image alt text: Descriptive, keyword where natural
- Schema markup: Article, FAQ, Product

**Step 5: Add Media**
- Featured image (1200x630 for social sharing)
- In-post images (product shots, lifestyle)
- Infographics where relevant
- Video embeds if available

**Step 6: Publish & Promote**
- Publish on blog
- Share to social (all platforms)
- Include in next email newsletter
- Create Pinterest pins from images
- Repurpose into social content

### Lookbook Creation

1. **Plan Theme**: Season, occasion, or collection
2. **Curate Products**: 8-15 items that work together
3. **Create Visuals**: Professional photography or Canva layouts
4. **Write Copy**: Brief, lifestyle-focused descriptions
5. **Build Page**: Dedicated landing page or PDF
6. **Add Shoppability**: Link every item to product page
7. **Promote**: Email, social, Pinterest, ads

## Common Mistakes

### âŒ Avoid These

1. **Writing for search engines, not humans** - Quality and readability matter
2. **No keyword strategy** - Random posts don't rank
3. **Thin content** - Under 1000 words rarely ranks
4. **Ignoring intent** - Match content to search intent

8. **Forgetting CTAs** - Every post needs next step
