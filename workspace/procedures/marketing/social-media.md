## Step-by-Step Process

### Daily Tasks (30 min)
1. **Engagement Block** (15 min)
   - Reply to ALL comments within 4 hours
   - Reply to ALL DMs within 24 hours
   - Like and comment on 10 posts from target hashtags
   - Save UGC content for repurposing

2. **Story Posting** (15 min)
   - Post 3-5 Stories daily
   - Mix: Product showcases, behind-scenes, polls, customer features
   - Use link stickers to drive traffic

### Weekly Tasks (2-3 hours)

1. **Content Creation Day** (Pick one day)
   - Batch-create 7-14 posts
   - Create 3-5 Reels/TikToks
   - Design carousel posts for engagement
   - Schedule using Later or Hootsuite

2. **Weekly Content Mix**
   | Day | Content Type | Goal |
   |-----|-------------|------|
   | Mon | Motivational/lifestyle | Engagement |
   | Tue | Product showcase | Sales |
   | Wed | Tips/educational | Value |
   | Thu | Behind-the-scenes | Connection |
   | Fri | Customer feature/UGC | Social proof |
   | Sat | Trending content | Reach |
   | Sun | Lifestyle/family moments | Brand |

3. **Analytics Review** (30 min)
   - Export weekly metrics
   - Identify top 3 performing posts
   - Note patterns (time, type, hashtags)
   - Adjust next week's strategy

### Monthly Tasks

1. **Content Audit**
   - Review all posts for the month
   - Calculate engagement rate: (likes + comments + shares + saves) / followers √ó 100
   - Document winning formulas
   - Archive underperforming content types

2. **Hashtag Research**
   - Update hashtag sets (3 sets of 25-30 hashtags)
   - Mix: 10 small (<50k), 10 medium (50k-500k), 10 large (>500k)
   - Track which sets perform best

3. **Competitor Analysis**
   - Check 5 competitors' top posts
   - Note new content formats
   - Identify gaps in their strategy to exploit

## Common Mistakes

### ‚ùå Mistakes to Avoid
1. **Posting and ghosting** - Post, then immediately engage for 15 min
2. **Ignoring DMs** - DMs are sales opportunities, respond fast
3. **Generic hashtags** - "#love #fashion" brings bots, not buyers
4. **Inconsistent posting** - Algorithm punishes gaps
5. **Only selling** - 80% value, 20% promotion
6. **Ignoring trends** - Trends = free reach
7. **Low-quality images** - Investing in photos pays 10x
8. **No call-to-action** - Tell people what to do
9. **Not using Stories** - Stories drive DMs and loyalty
10. **Forgetting captions** - Captions drive saves and shares

## Expert Tips ($10M Store Tactics)

### üéØ Advanced Strategies

1. **Create "micro-moments"**
   - Film EVERYTHING when shooting content
   - Those 3-second clips become gold for Stories/Reels

2. **Build a UGC army**
   - Incentivize customers to tag you
   - Offer $10 credit for usable UGC
   - Feature customers prominently = more UGC

3. **DM Funnel Strategy**
   - Post teaser ‚Üí "DM 'MATCH' for link"
   - This bypasses algorithm AND builds list
   - Auto-respond with ManyChat

4. **Collab Posts**
   - Reach both audiences
   - Do with influencers, customers, complementary brands
   - Free cross-promotion

5. **Pin strategic posts**
   - Top 3 pins should be: Best seller, Social proof, Current offer
   - Rotate monthly

6. **Story Highlights = Mini Website**
   - New Arrivals
   - Best Sellers
   - Reviews
   - Size Guide
   - About Us
   - FAQ

7. **Engagement before posting**
   - 30 min before posting, engage heavily
   - Algorithm rewards active users

8. **Recycling content**
   - Top posts from 3+ months ago can be reposted
   - Turn carousels into Reels
   - Turn Reels into Stories
   - Turn everything into Pinterest Pins

## Emergency Playbook

### If engagement drops suddenly:
1. Check for algorithm changes (Google "Instagram algorithm [date]")
2. Review last 10 posts - anything different?
3. Do a Story poll to re-engage followers
4. Go Live for 10 minutes (algorithm boost)
5. Post a Reel with trending audio

### If you get a viral post:
1. Drop everything and engage with every comment
2. Post a follow-up within 24 hours
3. Pin the post immediately
4. Cross-post to all platforms
5. Create an ad from it
