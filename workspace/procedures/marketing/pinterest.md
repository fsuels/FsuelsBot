## Why Pinterest for Mommy-and-Me

### The Pinterest Advantage
- **97%** of searches are unbranded (people discover new brands)
- **85%** of weekly pinners have purchased from Pinterest
- **Evergreen content** - Pins last months/years vs hours on other platforms
- **Purchase intent** - People plan purchases here
- **Visual product** = Perfect platform fit
- **Lower competition** than Instagram/Facebook

### Pinterest User Profile
- Primarily women (60%+)
- Ages 25-44 (prime mommy demo)
- Higher income than average social user
- Planning: weddings, holidays, photoshoots, nurseries

## Content Strategy

### Pin Types

| Pin Type | Purpose | Frequency |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| Product Pins | Direct sales | 60% of content |
| Idea Pins | Engagement/reach | 20% of content |
| Video Pins | Engagement | 10% of content |
| Repins | Community/board filling | 10% of content |

### Product Pin Best Practices

**Image Requirements**
- **Aspect ratio**: 2:3 (1000 x 1500 px)
- **File type**: PNG or JPEG
- **Quality**: High-res, well-lit
- **Style**: Lifestyle > flat lay

**Image Optimization**
- Show product in use (mom + child matching)
- Include face for emotion/connection
- Minimal text overlay (Pinterest prefers image-first)
- If text: readable, brand colors, <20% of image

**Pin Description Formula**
```
Hook statement with primary keyword.
2-3 sentences describing the product/benefit with secondary keywords naturally included.
Call-to-action (Shop now, Get the look, etc.)
3-5 relevant hashtags (yes, hashtags work on Pinterest)
```

### Idea Pins Strategy

Idea Pins = Pinterest's answer to Stories/Reels
- Multi-page format (up to 20 slides)
- Native to Pinterest (don't link out)
- Higher reach than standard Pins
- Great for engagement and followers

**Idea Pin Formats That Work**
1. "5 Ways to Style Matching Outfits"
2. "Get Ready With Us" (GRWM)
3. "Before & After" transformations
4. "Day in Our Life" (matching outfits featured)
5. "Outfit Ideas for [Occasion]"
6. "How to Choose Matching Sizes"

### Video Pins
- 6-60 seconds (15-30 optimal)
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Works well with audio off (add text)
- Same 2:3 aspect ratio

## Step-by-Step Process

### Daily Tasks (20 min)
1. **Pin Fresh Content** (10 min)
   - 5-10 new pins daily
   - Mix of product and content pins
   - Save to relevant boards

2. **Engagement** (10 min)
   - Reply to comments
   - Check messages
   - Engage with related content

### Weekly Tasks (2 hours)

1. **Content Creation Day**
   - Batch-create 20-30 pins
   - Design in Canva (Pinterest templates)
   - Write descriptions in advance
   - Schedule using Tailwind or Later

2. **Analytics Review**
   - Check top-performing pins
   - Note patterns (style, keywords)
   - Identify boards gaining traction

3. **Keyword Research**
   - Use Pinterest search bar (auto-suggestions)
   - Check Pinterest Trends
   - Update descriptions with new keywords

4. **Repin Strategy**
   - Repin your own content to different boards
   - Pin complementary content (not competitors)

### Monthly Tasks

1. **Board Audit**
   - Remove underperforming boards
   - Add seasonal boards
   - Reorganize board order (best performers first)

2. **Seasonal Planning**
   - Pinterest users plan 2-3 months ahead
   - Start Valentine's content in November
   - Start Christmas content in September
   - Create seasonal boards early

3. **Catalog Sync Check**
   - Verify all products syncing correctly
   - Fix any Rich Pin errors
   - Update product info as needed

**Primary Keywords** (high volume)
- Mommy and me outfits
- Matching mother daughter dresses
- Family matching clothes
- Mom and daughter matching

**Secondary Keywords** (more specific)
- Mommy and me photoshoot outfits
- Matching family pajamas
- Mother daughter birthday outfit
- Twinning outfit ideas

**Long-tail Keywords** (low competition, high intent)
- Pink mommy and me dresses for Easter
- Matching Christmas pajamas family of 4
- Affordable mommy and me outfits

### Where to Use Keywords
1. **Profile name and bio**
2. **Board titles and descriptions**
3. **Pin titles** (100 character max)
4. **Pin descriptions** (500 characters)
5. **Image file names** (before upload)
6. **Alt text** (for accessibility and SEO)

### Keyword Research Process
1. Type keyword in Pinterest search bar
2. Note auto-complete suggestions
3. Click search, note "Related searches" bubbles
4. Use Pinterest Trends for volume data
5. Build keyword spreadsheet by topic

## Pinterest Ads

### When to Start Ads
- After 30+ organic pins
- After enabling catalog
- After understanding what content works
- Budget: Start with $10-20/day

### Campaign Types

| Campaign | Objective | Use Case |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Awareness | Impressions | Brand building |
| Consideration | Traffic, engagement | Pre-launch, testing |
| Conversions | Sales | Main revenue driver |
| Catalog Sales | Product-specific sales | Shopping ads |

### Targeting Options

**Keywords** (most important)
- Use your organic keyword research
- Start with 25-50 keywords per campaign
- Mix broad and exact match

**Interests**
- Women's fashion
- Kids fashion
- Parenting
- Photography

**Audiences**
- Website visitors (retargeting)
- Email list upload
- Engagement audience (engaged with your pins)
- Actalikes (Pinterest lookalikes)

### Ad Creative
- Use top-performing organic pins
- Video ads perform well
- Carousel ads for multiple products
- Keep branding subtle

## Expert Tips ($10M Store Tactics)

### ðŸŽ¯ Advanced Strategies

1. **The 80/20 Content Rule**
   - 80% evergreen (works year-round)
   - 20% seasonal (holiday, events)
   - Evergreen = compound traffic over time

2. **Repin Your Own Content**
   - Save product pins to multiple relevant boards
   - Spreads content, signals relevance
   - Wait 2+ days between repins

3. **Idea Pin to Product Pin Pipeline**
   - Create Idea Pin (gets reach)
   - Point viewers to related Product Pin
   - Captures the discovery-to-purchase journey

4. **The "Fresh Pin" Algorithm**
   - Pinterest loves new content
   - Same product, new image = fresh pin
   - Create 3-5 pin variations per product

5. **Rich Pins = Trust Signals**
   - Price, availability shown on pin
   - Higher click-through rates
   - Keep product feed clean and synced

6. **Catalog Section Organization**
   - Organize product groups
   - Enables better Shopping ads
   - Improves browse experience

7. **Pin at Peak Times**
   - Best times: 8-11 PM, weekends
   - Pinterest is evening/planning activity
   - Test and track for your audience

8. **Collaborate Boards**
   - Partner with complementary brands
   - Share audiences
   - More content, less work
