# Reporting Procedure

## Revenue Impact
Weekly metrics review = catch problems fast, double down on winners

---

## Daily Check (5 min)

| Metric | Where | Action if Off |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| Sales today | Shopify Home | Check ads, site issues |
| Ad spend | Meta/Google | Pause if ROAS bad |
| Orders | Shopify Orders | Fulfill quickly |

## Monthly Review (1 hour)

### All Weekly Metrics PLUS:

**Customer Metrics**
- New vs returning customer revenue split
- Repeat purchase rate
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Customer lifetime value (LTV)

**Profitability**
- Gross revenue
- COGS
- Ad spend
- Other costs
- Net profit

**Trends**
- MoM revenue growth
- MoM traffic growth
- Best performing channel
- Biggest problem area

---

## Where to Find Data

| Metric | Source |
|--------|--------|
| Sales, orders, AOV | Shopify Analytics |
| Traffic, conversion | GA4 |
| Ad performance | Meta/Google/Pinterest dashboards |
| Email | Klaviyo/ESP analytics |
| Profit | Spreadsheet calculation |

---

## Red Flags (Act Immediately)

- Conversion rate drops >20%
- ROAS drops below 1.5x
- Cart abandonment spikes
- Negative reviews increase
- Site speed degrades

---

## Quick Win

**This Week**: Set up a simple Google Sheet with weekly metrics. Track for 4 weeks, then you'll see patterns. Data without action is useless - use it to make decisions.
