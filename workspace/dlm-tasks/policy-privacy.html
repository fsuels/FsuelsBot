2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you shop with us, create an account, or get in touch, you may provide:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number
  • Shipping and billing addresses
  • Payment information — credit/debit card details (processed securely by Shopify Payments; we never see or store your full card number)
  • Account credentials — email and password if you create an account
  • Communications — messages you send us via email, contact forms, or social media
  • Order details — products purchased, sizes, quantities

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our site, certain information is collected automatically:

  • Device and browser information — browser type, operating system, screen resolution, device type
  • IP address and approximate location (city/region level)
  • Browsing activity — pages visited, products viewed, time spent on pages, referring website
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 5)

Information from Third Parties

We may receive limited information from:

  • Shopify — our e-commerce platform, which processes orders and provides site analytics
  • Payment processors — transaction confirmation and fraud prevention data
  • Marketing platforms — if you interact with our ads or email campaigns

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and receipts
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Improve our website, products, and shopping experience
  • Send marketing emails (only with your consent)
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, accounting, law enforcement)
  • Personalize your shopping experience

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party services to run our store. Each has access only to the information necessary to perform their function:

  • Shopify — powers our online store, processes orders, and provides analytics
  • Shopify Payments (powered by Stripe) — securely processes credit card payments. Your payment data is encrypted and handled in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.
  • Email marketing provider — if you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is shared with our email marketing service. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Shipping carriers — your name, address, and phone number are shared with carriers to deliver your order and provide tracking.
  • Google Analytics — we may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

We do not share your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. We use them to:

  • Keep your shopping cart active between visits
  • Remember your preferences (language, currency)
  • Understand site traffic and improve performance
  • Support marketing efforts (with your consent)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to shop on our site.

6. Children's Privacy

Dress Like Mommy sells children's clothing, but our website is not directed at children. All purchases must be made by a parent, guardian, or adult (18 years or older).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16 (or 13 in the United States). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at info@dresslikemommy.com and we will promptly delete it.

7. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary:

  • Order and transaction records — retained for 7 years for tax and accounting compliance
  • Account information — retained while your account is active; deleted upon request
  • Marketing consent records — retained until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics data — identifiable data is deleted or anonymized within 26 months
  • Customer support communications — retained for up to 3 years after resolution

8. Your Rights

For All Customers

Regardless of where you live, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Request deletion of your personal data

GDPR Rights (EEA, UK, Switzerland)

You also have the right to data portability, restrict processing, object to processing, and withdraw consent. You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

CCPA/CPRA Rights (California Residents)

You have the right to know what personal information we collect, delete your data, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@dresslikemommy.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

10. International Data Transfers
