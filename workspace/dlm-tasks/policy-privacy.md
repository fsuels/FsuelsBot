## 4. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party services to run our store. Each has access only to the information necessary to perform their function:

- **Shopify** — powers our online store, processes orders, and provides analytics about site usage. [Shopify's Privacy Policy](https://www.shopify.com/legal/privacy)
- **Shopify Payments (powered by Stripe)** — securely processes credit card payments. Your payment data is encrypted and handled in compliance with PCI-DSS standards. We never store your full credit card number on our servers.
- **Email marketing provider** — if you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is shared with our email marketing service to deliver campaigns. You can unsubscribe at any time.
- **Shipping carriers** — your name, address, and phone number are shared with carriers to deliver your order and provide tracking.
- **Google Analytics** — we may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

We do not share your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

## 5. Cookies and Tracking

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. We use them to:

- **Keep your shopping cart active** between visits
- **Remember your preferences** (language, currency)
- **Understand site traffic** and improve performance
- **Support marketing efforts** (with your consent)

### Types of Cookies We Use

| Type | Purpose | Required? |
|---|---|---|
| Essential | Shopping cart, checkout, security | Yes |
| Analytics | Site performance, visitor statistics | Optional |
| Marketing | Ad relevance, campaign measurement | Optional |

### Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to shop on our site.

## 8. Your Rights

### For All Customers

Regardless of where you live, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Unsubscribe** from marketing emails at any time (click the link in any email, or email us)
- **Request deletion** of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)

### Additional Rights Under GDPR (European Economic Area, UK, Switzerland)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you also have the right to:

- **Data portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Restrict processing** — ask us to limit how we use your data
- **Object to processing** — object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Withdraw consent** — at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
- **Lodge a complaint** with your local data protection authority

### Additional Rights Under CCPA/CPRA (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

- **Know** what personal information we collect, use, and disclose
- **Delete** your personal information
- **Opt out of the sale** of personal information — **we do not sell your personal information**
- **Non-discrimination** — we will not treat you differently for exercising your rights
- **Correct** inaccurate personal information
- **Limit the use of sensitive personal information** — we only use sensitive information for purposes necessary to provide our services

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **info@dresslikemommy.com** or write to us at the address listed above. We will respond within 30 days (or 45 days for complex CCPA requests, with notice).
